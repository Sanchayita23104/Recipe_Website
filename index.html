Matar Paneer Recipe



Ingredients:


  • 1 cup paneer cubes
  • 1 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup cashew nuts (soaked in warm water for 15 minutes)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure:


  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add green chilies and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and the oil starts to separate.
  5. Let the cooked mixture cool slightly, then blend it into a smooth paste.
  6. In the same pan, add the blended paste and cook for a few minutes until it thickens.
  7. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Add yogurt, stirring continuously to avoid curdling. Add the cashew paste and mix well.
  9. Add green peas and paneer cubes. Stir gently to coat the peas and paneer with the curry.
  10. Cover the pan and simmer the curry for 10-15 minutes until the peas and paneer are cooked through.
  11. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  12. Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.

Chicken Curry Recipe



Ingredients:


  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedure:


  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and chopped green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and oil starts to separate.
  5. Stir in turmeric powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add chicken pieces and brown them evenly.
  7. Whisk yogurt and add it to the pan. Mix well and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  9. Cover and cook until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  10. Adjust seasoning and serve hot with rice or naan.
